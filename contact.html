My Dream Places to Visit

Singapore and Shibuya, Japan are my dream places to visit. I’ve always been fascinated by their vibrant streets, bustling energy, and unique cultures.

In Singapore, I imagine exploring stunning gardens, futuristic architecture, and tasting amazing food from all over the world.

In Shibuya, Japan, I can imagine myself wandering through the busy streets, enjoying the neon lights, and experiencing the excitement of the famous Shibuya Crossing.

These places feel full of life and adventure, and visiting them has always been something I dream of doing someday.

Life of an IT Student

Being a 1st year BSIT student is both exciting and a little overwhelming.

I’m learning so many new things about computers, coding, and technology, and trying to keep up with projects and assignments.

It’s a lot to handle, but I’m enjoying every bit of the experience and looking forward to what’s ahead.

I realized that being an BSIT student requires patience, creativity, and problem-solving skills.
